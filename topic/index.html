What is an options contract?

An options contract is a type of financial instrument which gives the buyer an option to buy or sell at a certain price at or before a certain date. So why are options contracts so important? Well, options are based off a few principles which will be discussed in the other pages of this website.

The most important aspect of options is that they allow an investor to control a certain amount of an underlying asset without directly investing proportionally. Therefore, options can be used to both hedge risk, or magnify returns.
